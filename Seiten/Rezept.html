Linzer Cookies instructions

Ingredients: For this wonderful Linzer cookies recipe you need the following ingredients: Soft butter, powdered sugar, salt, vanilla sugar, water, flour, redcurrant jelly.

Step 2: Place 175g of softened butter into a mixing bowl

Step 3: Lightly break the butter into smaller pieces to make mixing easier.

Step 4: Add 80 grams of powdered sugar to the butter

Step 5: Add a pinch of salt, a teaspoon of vanilla sugar, and 1-2 tablespoons of water to the mixture.

Step 6: Gradually add 250 grams of flour to the mixture while continuously mixing. Ensure everything is well combined.

Step 7: Knead the mixture until it forms a smooth dough, as shown in the image.

Step 8: Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest in the refrigerator for approximately 30 minutes

Step 9:Roll out the dough evenly on a lightly floured work surface until it is about 3-4 mm thick. Cut out the bottoms of the cookies using cookie cutters of your choice. Use the same shapes for the tops, but also cut a small hole or pattern in the middle.

Step 10: Carefully place the cut-out pieces of dough on a baking tray lined with baking paper. Make sure that the shapes remain intact and that there is enough space between the cookies.

Step 11: Bake the linzer cookies in the middle of the oven at 200 °C for about 5-10 minutes until they are lightly golden brown.

Spread the underside of the Linzer cookies with jam. Then carefully place the tops on top and sprinkle with powdered sugar to taste. When you are finished, enjoy it!
